Mr. Scheveck and his twin brother are the owners and partners of Scheveck Law. He is a persistent litigator focusing on issues involving criminal law, personal injury, family law, expungements, and bankruptcy. Mr. Scheveck brings a unique perspective to family law issues. Lyndon and Layne primarily focus on men's rights. We believe that men's rights are just as important.

Before going to law school he served as a social worker for the state of Idaho. He graduated from Dakota Wesleyan University in Mitchell, South Dakota with his bachelors in Business Administration with a concentration in accounting. He went on to earn his Masters degree in Education. Mr. Scheveck later went to get his juris doctorate from Appalachian School of Law in Grundy, Virginia. Where he was a member of the trial team and earned the nationally known certificate in natural resources.

During law school he worked at the Yellowstone County Courthouse and worked as a landman for oil and gas companies. Lyndon grew up in Billings, Montana and is a member of the Montana State Bar, North Dakota State Bar, and is admitted to practice in the United States District Court of Montana.

When Lyndon is not in his office, you can always find him teaching, coaching at the local school or gym. You can also find him on one of Montana's many rivers fly fishing.






Layne Scheveck's passion for family law began early in his career as a student at Dakota Wesleyan University, where he interned at MorganTheeler PLLC, taking an interest in family law. During his legal studies at Cooley Law School, Layne concentrated his time with seminars in family law. Prior to starting Scheveck Law Firm, Layne worked at the Montana Public Defender's Office and as a private attorney, where he represented defendants from simple assaults to the most serious felonies. Layne specializes in family law, criminal law, and personal injury in motorcycle accidents.

Prior to attending law school, Layne earned his BA in Business with an emphasis on accounting.

Layne is very happy to be back in his home town. When he is not working on behalf of clients, Layne enjoys all that Montana mountains have to offer.
