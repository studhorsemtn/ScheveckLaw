The Scheveck Law Firm, PLLC is a full-service law firm located in downtown Billings, MT. We focus our practice on the areas of criminal defense, personal injury, family law, bankruptcy, and oil & gas law. We have attorneys licensed to practice in Federal District Court and all Montana State Courts, as well as all state courts in North Dakota.







Lyndon Scheveck is an owner and partner of Scheveck Law Firm, PLLP. He is a persistent litigator focusing on issues involving criminal law, personal injury, family law, expungements, bankruptcy, and oil & gas. Mr. Scheveck brings a unique perspective to the law. He strongly believes in the constitution and that everyone has a right to be heard and judged in front of his peers.

He graduated from Dakota Wesleyan University in Mitchell, South Dakota where he earned his bachelors degree in Business Administration and his Masters degree in Education. Mr. Scheveck later went to get his juris doctorate from Appalachian School Law in Grundy, Virginia. Where he was a member of the criminal defense trial team and earned a certificate in natural resources. During law school he worked at the Yellowstone County Courthouse and worked as a Landman for oil companies. Lyndon grew up in Billings, Montana and is a member of the Montana State Bar, North Dakota State, and is admitted to practice in the United States Federal District Court of Montana.

When Lyndon is not in his office, you can always find him coaching at the local gym. You can also find him on one of Montana's many rivers fly fishing.






Layne Scheveck's passion for family law began early in his career as a student at Dakota Wesleyan University, where he interned at MorganTheeler PLLC, taking an interest in family law. During his legal studies at Cooley Law School, Layne concentrated his time with seminars in family law. Prior to starting Scheveck Law Firm, Layne worked at the Montana Public Defender's Office and as a private attorney, where he represented defendants from simple assaults to the most serious felonies. Layne specializes in family law, criminal law, and personal injury in motorcycle accidents.

Prior to attending law school, Layne earned his BA in Business with an emphasis on accounting.

Layne is very happy to be back in his home town. When he is not working on behalf of clients, Layne enjoys all that Montana mountains have to offer.
