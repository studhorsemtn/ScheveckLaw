Personal Injury



We understand that the issues our clients face when they have been injured due to the negligence of others. Every case is unique and that each case requires different skills and approaches. A personal injury of any kind can be an extremely traumatic event when you or family member is injured due to the negligence of others. From the initial shock of the accident to the reality of living with the injuries sustained, accidents and negligent events resulting in serious personal injury can present one of life’s most stressful challenges. When you add the stress that comes from dealing with medical providers and insurance coverage, it is easy to see why experienced and effective personal injury attorneys are needed to obtain full compensation with any personal injury claim. Scheveck Law Firm is here to help if you have been injured or a loved one has been killed due to any of the following:

  •  Automobile/Motorcycle Accidents
  •  Wrongful Death
  •  Premises Liability
  •  Worker’s Compensation

We have experience in the field of personal injury litigation, and we regularly try cases throughout Montana as well as in neighboring jurisdictions. If you have been injured due to the negligence of others, it is important that you contact an attorney right away. Most claims for negligence must be brought within a specific time period or your claim will be forever barred. Criminal Law



If you are under investigation for criminal activity or have been charged with a crime or violation of traffic laws, then you need an attorney with extensive experience handling these matters who can provide you with effective representation and counseling. The Scheveck Law Firm criminal defense attorneys have experience providing aggressive representation, who have been charged with crimes ranging from simple misdemeanors to serious felonies in both state and federal court, throughout Montana and neighboring jurisdictions. If you or a loved one is facing criminal charges, it is important to consult with our lawyers who have experience handling cases from domestic violence all the way up to rape. Our team has seen it all, and tried it all with success.
