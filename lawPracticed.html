Scheveck Law

Oil & Gas Litigation

The Scheveck Law Firm counsels regional and national natural gas and coal-bed methane gas owners, producers, and drillers. We assist with permitting, leasing, property acquisitions, mediation, arbitration, litigation, and we represent various parties involved in disputes as to coal-bed methane ownership pending in state and federal courts. We have experience representing companies and individuals in the broader energy sector, along with the relationships and contacts that we have developed, give us a unique perspective as we work with our clients to meet the challenges of gas and oil exploration and production.
