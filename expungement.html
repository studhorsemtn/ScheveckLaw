Mistakes? We could clear your record. We could change your life.

Have you ever been embarrassed because of your past? Have you been denied a job because of your criminal record? Have you ever had your gun rights taken away? Do you think your past reflects on who you are today? One mistake that you made years ago can follow and wreak chaos on you for years. Your criminal record can prevent you from living the life you want.

Ninety-two percent of employers conduct background checks on job applicants, and that one small mistake can stop you from your dream job or even the ability to provide food for your family.

The State of Montana has recently overhauled their expungement laws and the Scheveck Law Firm has created a fast and easy online way to erase those mistakes. Misdemeanor convictions in Montana are now available for expungements including DUIs, reckless driving, PFMA, (Partner Family Member Assault), some drug possession convictions, and many more. Let us help you so you can get back to the life you want to live.

The Scheveck Law Firm was created to safeguard Montanans who are eligible to clear their record and give them a chance to do so. To see if you qualify to have your convictions erased, complete the free online assessment and an experienced criminal defense attorney will review your record. You will then be contacted with a range of affordable options to clear your record.
